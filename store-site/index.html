Privacy Policy

Landfall collects nothing. There is no account, no server, no analytics SDK, and no network connection of any kind. Everything you log — the things you're working on, the time you spend, and any notes you write — stays only on your device, stored locally by the operating system.

  • No personal data is collected, transmitted, or shared with anyone, including the developer.
  • No tracking, advertising identifiers, or analytics.
  • No account or sign-in of any kind.
  • Photos you attach to a study item are stored only in the app's local data and are never uploaded anywhere.
  • If you delete the app, all of its data is deleted with it. There is nothing on any server to remove, because nothing ever left your device.

This policy may be updated if the app's functionality changes. Any future update will keep the same principle: your data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it yourself (for example, using the Share button to save or send an image you generated).
