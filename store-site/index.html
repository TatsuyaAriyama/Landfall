Privacy Policy

Your records live on your device. Signing in is optional and powers only two things: syncing your records across your own devices, and sharing a "harbor" with friends. Landfall never tracks you and never sells your data.

  • What we collect (only if you sign in): your email address and name (from Sign in with Apple or Google), a user ID, and your study content (items, times, notes, logged days). All of it is used solely to run the app's sync and harbor features — never for advertising or tracking.
  • Where it's stored: on your device, and — when signed in — in Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore). Traffic is encrypted.
  • Sharing: data is shared only when you join a harbor with an invite code, and only with that harbor's members (your current-month records). Leave the harbor and that shared copy is removed.
  • No tracking: no advertising identifiers, no third-party analytics or ad networks, no cross-app tracking.
  • Deletion: choose "Delete account" in Settings to permanently remove your account and all synced and shared data. If you use Landfall without signing in, nothing ever leaves your device.

The full policy (English and 日本語) is available in the app repository. This policy may be updated if the app's functionality changes.
